Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for industrial identification and safety.

Electrical & Traffolyte Engraving

We provide precision laser engraving and rotary engraving for a wide range of electrical and industrial applications. Our labels are weather-resistant, durable, and customized to your exact specifications.

  • Switchboard Labels & Legend Plates
  • Control Panel Fascias
  • Asset & Equipment Identification Tags
  • Cable Markers & Core Tags
  • Valve Tags & Pipe Markers

Materials We Use:

Traffolyte (Lamacoid), Acrylic, Anodized Aluminum, Stainless Steel, and more.

UV Printed Safety & Custom Signs

Our direct-to-substrate UV printing technology produces vibrant, full-color signs that are scratch-resistant and suitable for indoor and outdoor use. From standard safety signs to fully custom branding, we deliver exceptional results.

  • Workplace Health & Safety (WHS) Signs
  • Danger, Warning, and Caution Signs
  • Fire & Emergency Signage
  • Custom Branded Site Signs
  • Machinery Decals & Operating Panels

Substrates Available:

Corflute, PVC, Aluminum Composite Panel (ACP), Acrylic, and more.
